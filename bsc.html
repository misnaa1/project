Bachelor of Science (BSc)

Overview

The Bachelor of Science (BSc) is a 3-year undergraduate degree that offers a wide range of specializations in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) fields.

Types of BSc Courses

  • Full-Time BSc Course
  • Part-Time BSc Course
  • Distance BSc Course
  • Online BSc Course

BSc Specializations

  • BSc Physics
  • BSc Biotechnology
  • BSc Computer Science
  • BSc Mathematics

BSc Admission Process

Admissions to BSc courses are typically based on merit or entrance exams like CUET, NPAT, or SET.

Some top BSc colleges in India include:

  • St. Stephen's College
  • Hindu College
  • Christ University

Career Opportunities

BSc graduates can pursue various career paths, including:

  • Research and Development
  • Industry and Manufacturing
  • Education and Academia
  • Government Jobs

Salary Range

The salary range for BSc graduates varies depending on the specialization, industry, and location.

Here are some approximate salary ranges:

  • Entry-level: ₹3-5 lakhs per annum
  • Mid-level: ₹6-10 lakhs per annum
  • Senior-level: ₹12-20 lakhs per annum
