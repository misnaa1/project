Nursing Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB
  • Qualified in entrance exams like NEET or state-level exams

Course Details

Duration: 4 years (B.Sc Nursing) Course Type: Undergraduate Specializations: Pediatric Nursing, Obstetric Nursing, Psychiatric Nursing, etc.

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Staff Nurse
  • Nursing Tutor
  • Nursing Superintendent
  • Nursing Researcher

Course Overview

The nursing course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of nursing principles, practices, and management. Students will learn about various aspects of nursing, including pediatric nursing, obstetric nursing, psychiatric nursing, and community health nursing.
