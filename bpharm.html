B.Pharm Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB/M
  • Qualified in entrance exams like NEET, JEE, or state-level exams

Course Details

Duration: 4 years Course Type: Undergraduate Specializations: Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, and Pharmacognosy

Career Opportunities

  • Pharmaceutical Research Scientist
  • Clinical Research Coordinator
  • Pharmaceutical Marketing Manager
  • Regulatory Affairs Specialist
  • Quality Control Manager

Course Overview

The B.Pharm course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of pharmaceutical sciences, including the development, formulation, and use of medications. Students will learn about various aspects of pharmacy, including pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmacology, and pharmacognosy.
