Bachelor of Arts (BA)

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) is a 3-year undergraduate degree course that focuses on the study of humanities, social sciences, and liberal arts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with any stream (Arts, Commerce, Science)
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate

Course Structure

The BA course is divided into 6 semesters, with a range of subjects to choose from, including:

  • English Literature
  • Hindi Literature
  • History
  • Political Science
  • Philosophy
  • Sociology
  • Psychology
  • Economics

Specializations

Some popular specializations in BA include:

  • BA English Honours
  • BA History Honours
  • BA Political Science Honours
  • BA Economics Honours

Career Opportunities

BA graduates can pursue careers in:

  • Teaching and education
  • Writing and journalism
  • Public administration and governance
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Human resources and management

Top Institutes

Some top institutes for BA include:

  • University of Delhi
  • Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)
  • University of Mumbai
  • University of Calcutta

Salary Range

The salary range for BA graduates varies based on experience and industry, but here are some approximate figures:

  • Entry-level: ₹ 2,50,000 - ₹ 4,00,000 per annum
  • Mid-level: ₹ 6,00,000 - ₹ 10,00,000 per annum
  • Senior-level: ₹ 15,00,000 - ₹ 25,00,000 per annum
