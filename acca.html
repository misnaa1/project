ACCA Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Commerce, Accounting, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • Registered with ACCA

Course Structure

The ACCA course is divided into three levels:

  • Knowledge Level: 3 papers that cover the basics of accounting, taxation, and auditing.
  • Skill Level: 6 papers that cover financial accounting, corporate laws, taxation, and auditing.
  • Professional Level: 4 papers that cover advanced accounting, auditing, and taxation.

Exams

The ACCA course has two types of exams:

  • Computer-Based Exams (CBEs): For Knowledge and Skill levels
  • Written Exams: For Professional level

Duration

The minimum duration of the ACCA course is 2-3 years, and the maximum duration is 5-6 years.
