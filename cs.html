Company Secretaryship (CS) Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Commerce, Accounting, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • Registered with the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI)

Course Structure

The CS course is divided into three levels:

  • Foundation Program: A 8-month course that covers the basics of company law, accounting, and management.
  • Executive Program: A 1-year course that covers company law, corporate governance, and financial management.
  • Professional Program: A 1-year course that covers advanced company law, corporate governance, and financial management.

Exams

The CS course has three levels of exams:

  • Foundation Exam: A single exam that tests the student's knowledge of the foundation program.
  • Executive Exam: A two-module exam that tests the student's knowledge of the executive program.
  • Professional Exam: A three-module exam that tests the student's knowledge of the professional program.

Duration

The minimum duration of the CS course is 2-3 years, and the maximum duration is 5-6 years.

The salary range for CS professionals varies based on experience and location:
