Cost and Management Accountancy (CMA) Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Commerce, Accounting, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • Registered with the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI)

Course Structure

The CMA course is divided into three levels:

  • Foundation Course: A 6-month course that covers the basics of accounting, taxation, and auditing.
  • Intermediate Course: A 1-year course that covers financial accounting, corporate laws, taxation, and auditing.
  • Final Course: A 1-year course that covers advanced accounting, auditing, and taxation.

Exams

The CMA course has three levels of exams:

  • Foundation Exam: A single exam that tests the student's knowledge of the foundation course.
  • Intermediate Exam: A two-group exam that tests the student's knowledge of the intermediate course.
  • Final Exam: A two-group exam that tests the student's knowledge of the final course.

Duration

The minimum duration of the CMA course is 2-3 years, and the maximum duration is 5-6 years.
