Chartered Accountancy (CA) Course Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Commerce, Accounting, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • Registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)

Course Structure

The CA course is divided into three levels:

  • Foundation Course: A 4-month course that covers the basics of accounting, taxation, and auditing.
  • Intermediate Course: A 8-month course that covers financial accounting, corporate laws, and taxation.
  • Final Course: A 10-month course that covers advanced accounting, auditing, and taxation.

Exams

The CA course has three levels of exams:

  • Foundation Exam: A single exam that tests the student's knowledge of the foundation course.
  • Intermediate Exam: A two-group exam that tests the student's knowledge of the intermediate course.
  • Final Exam: A two-group exam that tests the student's knowledge of the final course.

Duration

The minimum duration of the CA course is 3.5 years, and the maximum duration is 6 years.

Fees

Fees Amount
Registration Fee ₹ 9,000 - ₹ 10,000
Exam Fee ₹ 1,500 - ₹ 2,000 per exam
Course Material Fee ₹ 5,000 - ₹ 10,000

Career Opportunities

  • Chartered Accountant: A professional who provides accounting, auditing, and taxation services to clients.
  • Financial Analyst: A professional who analyzes financial data to help businesses make informed decisions.
  • Tax Consultant: A professional who provides tax planning and consulting services to clients.

Top Institutes

  • Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI): The primary institute for CA education in India.
  • Indian Institute of Management (IIM): Offers a PG program in management accounting that is equivalent to CA.
  • Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICAI): Offers a course in cost accounting that is equivalent to CA.

Salary Range

The salary range for CA professionals varies based on experience and location:

  • Entry-level: ₹ 6,00,000 - ₹ 10,00,000 per annum
  • Mid-level: ₹ 15,00,000 - ₹ 25,00,000 per annum
  • Senior-level: ₹ 30,00,000 - ₹ 50,00,000 per annum
