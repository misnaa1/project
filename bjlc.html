Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication (BJMC)

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication (BJMC) is a 3-year undergraduate degree course that focuses on the study of journalism, mass communication, and media studies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with any stream (Arts, Commerce, Science)
  • Minimum 50% marks in aggregate
  • Some colleges may require an entrance exam or interview

Course Structure

The BJMC course is divided into 6 semesters, with a range of subjects to choose from, including:

  • Introduction to Journalism and Mass Communication
  • Reporting and Writing for Media
  • Media Studies and Research
  • Advertising and Public Relations
  • Broadcasting and Digital Media
  • Media Ethics and Law

Specializations

Some popular specializations in BJMC include:

  • Print Journalism
  • Broadcast Journalism
  • Digital Media
  • Public Relations
  • Advertising

Career Opportunities

BJMC graduates can pursue careers in:

  • Journalism and reporting
  • Public relations and corporate communications
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Digital media and online content creation
  • Broadcasting and television production

Top Institutes

Some top institutes for BJMC include:

  • Indian Institute of Mass Communication (IIMC), Delhi
  • Mass Communication Research Centre (MCRC), Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi
  • Symbiosis Institute of Media and Communication (SIMC), Pune
  • Mudra Institute of Communications (MICA), Ahmedabad

Salary Range

The salary range for BJMC graduates varies based on experience and industry, but here are some approximate figures:

  • Entry-level: ₹ 2,00,000 - ₹ 4,00,000 per annum
  • Mid-level: ₹ 5,00,000 - ₹ 10,00,000 per annum
  • Senior-level: ₹ 15,00,000 - ₹ 25,00,000 per annum
